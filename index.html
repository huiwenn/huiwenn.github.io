Hi! Sophia / 孙慧文 here.

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at UC San Diego, advised by prof. Rose Yu, working on machine learning for spatiotemporal data, uncertainty quantification of ML models, and decision making under uncertainty.

I obtained my B.A. in mathematics and computer science at Wellesley College. In summer 2018 I interned at Google DeepMind to work on ML for healthcare, and in summer 2020 at Amazon Robotics to work on reinforcement learning for multi-robot planning.

I'm also interested in machine creativity and digital humanities.
